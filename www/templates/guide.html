Using GMAT Reminder

Just five steps:
  1. Select a specific time of the day that is most convenient to receive reminder for your practice (this time can only be changed once per day)
  2. Slide the 'Deactivate Reminder' toggle if you decide later to completely stop daily notifications
  3. Install GMAT Veritas Mobile App
  4. Using the username and password provided in the 'Home' tab, log into the GMAT Veritas App that you downloaded.
  5. Tap 'Refresh' button to view your metrics and rewards.
